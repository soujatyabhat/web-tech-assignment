BITS Pilani

Founded in 1964, Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani stands tall as one of India's premier engineering and science institutions. Nestled amidst the rustic charm of Pilani, Rajasthan, BITS Pilani has carved a niche for itself in the realm of higher education, fostering innovation, and excellence. With a rich legacy of academic brilliance and a commitment to holistic development, BITS Pilani continues to nurture generations of thinkers, innovators, and leaders, shaping the future of technology and beyond.
